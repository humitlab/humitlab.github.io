Human U Machine Intelligent Touch (HUMIT) Lab

人机触觉智能实验室

The multidisciplinary research team at HUMIT Lab is dedicated to advancing haptic science and technology. We explore the information space underlying human touch perception, innovate tactile sensing systems that empower intelligent machines with refined touch dexterity, and design haptic interfaces that seamlessly connect the physical and digital worlds. We aim to bridge the gap between humans, cyberspace, and machines by building a unified framework for next-generation haptic technologies.
